## Writing rules for all 10 pages
- avoid fluff
- prioritize commercial usefulness over generic blog language
- keep tables and bullets easy to scan
- include a clear verdict and who the page is for
- link to vendor pages, compare hub, and quiz
- do not make precise unsupported claims
